What is Heartfulness?

Heartfulness is a practical approach to heart-based meditation rooted in the ancient tradition of Raja Yoga. It's been refined and simplified for modern everyday life — anyone can learn it regardless of background, culture, or belief.

What makes Heartfulness unique is pranahuti — yogic transmission — a subtle energy offered by certified trainers during every session. It takes your meditation beyond what the mind can achieve alone, allowing you to experience deep states of consciousness from the very first sitting.

Heartfulness Chicago is a chapter of the global Heartfulness Institute — a not-for-profit organization active in over 130 countries. Every session, workshop, and program we offer is completely free.

The Heartfulness Way

Four Simple Daily Practices

A simple set of relaxation and meditation practices — dynamic and effective tools for personal growth. Practice them daily and feel the shift.

01

Relaxation

Heartfulness Relaxation removes stress and tension from the body, calms the nervous system, and settles your breathing — so you arrive at deep ease before meditation begins.

Simple enough for a 5-year-old to learn, yet profoundly effective for even the most stressed adult. It's how we start every session at our Chicago center.

Releases accumulated tension
Prepares body and mind for meditation
02

Meditation

Suppose there is a divine light in your heart. With the gentle feeling of that light, the practice begins — and through pranahuti (yogic transmission), the experience deepens beyond what the mind can achieve alone.

This is what makes Heartfulness different from other meditation approaches. Our trained practitioners transmit during every session across all five Chicagoland locations.

Unique yogic transmission (pranahuti)
Deep states from the very first session
03

Cleaning (Rejuvenation)

Heartfulness Cleaning purifies the mind of all its complexities and heaviness. Practiced in the evening, it detoxes the impressions accumulated throughout the day.

You'll feel lighter, sleep better, and wake up with more clarity. It's the practice our members say changed their relationship with daily stress.

Daily mental detox in the evening
Better sleep and rejuvenation
04

Inner Connect (Prayer)

At bedtime, Heartfulness Inner Connect allows you to listen to your heart's voice and connect with something deeper within. Like taking a dip into the source of life — you emerge refreshed and balanced.

Through this quiet practice, you develop a profound sense of purpose and direction that carries into everything you do.

Deepens self-awareness and intuition
A peaceful way to close each day
